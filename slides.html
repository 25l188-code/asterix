The Challenge

The Reach vs. Ecological Impact Dilemma

ACS / 02
  • Ground Patrolling Impact: Heavy standard ATVs cause critical soil compaction, destroy sensitive forest root networks, and disturb wildlife feeding/mating beats with exhaust and loud engine noises.
  • Aerial Patrolling Limitations: Commercial drones provide excellent line-of-sight reach but suffer from short flight times (20–30 mins), tiny payload capacities, and cannot take physical soil or river samples.
  • Isolation & Blindspots: Thick forest foliage blocks GPS and radio lines of sight, leaving rangers blind to poaching or wild fires in deep valleys.
🌲

Zero-Disturbance

How do we protect deep forest regions without entering them with heavy machinery?

The Innovation

The Cooperative Ground-Air Swarm Solution

ACS / 03
  • A Shared Ecosystem: Moving away from a standalone buggy. We propose a mobile coordinate system combining an AEOUV (ground hub) and EcoWing-U drones (aerial extensions).
  • Role of the AEOUV: Stays strictly on designated forest logging trails. Serves as a mobile charging grid, computing core, robotic sampler, and drone airport.
  • Role of the Drones: Dispatched dynamically from the vehicle to deploy sensors, drop seeds, or administer vaccines in trackless valleys.

Aerial Platform

EcoWing-U: Universal Conservation Drone

ACS / 06
  • Hybrid VTOL Design: Vertical rotors for ascending through tight forest canopies, switching to rear pusher propeller and folding wings for efficient 25 km forward cruise flight.
  • Single Manufacturing Line: All drones share the identical airframe, flight computers, navigation sensors, and batteries, reducing spare parts overhead.
  • Full Mission Redundancy: Drones have no fixed role. If Drone 1 is damaged, Drone 2 immediately clips onto the same Mission Pod to resume the task.

Key Mechanical Innovation

Adaptive Mission Bay (AMB)

ACS / 07
  • Automated Payload Swapping: Fully enclosed, weatherproof internal compartment storing a magazine of six Mission Pods.
  • Robotic Linear Shuttle: Replaces payloads in under 30 seconds without human hands.
  • Universal Interface: Uses high-strength electro-permanent alignment magnets, pairing power and data contacts instantly.
  • Sealed Roof Hatches: Roof hatches open only during launch/landing to keep internal electronics free from mud and rain.
