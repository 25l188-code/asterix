Tender Bid Summary

Chassis & Mechanicals

  • Frame: High-strength tubular steel space frame (aBAJA compliant).
  • Suspension: Double-wishbone off-road suspension with 8-inch travel.
  • Dimensions: Width: 162 cm (64 in) | Track-friendly for walking beats.
  • Tires: High-traction 23-inch knobby mud terrain tires.

Powertrain & Battery

  • Voltage System: 72V Nominal Category II Tractive System.
  • Motor: 9 kW Brushless DC (BLDC) Motor with regenerative braking.
  • Battery: AIS 156 Certified Lithium-Ion Pack (80 Ah Capacity).
  • Acoustics: Extremely silent patrol mode (≤ 70 dB).

Autonomy & Perception

  • Supervisory Controller: Nvidia Jetson Orin Nano Developer Kit.
  • Sensors: Roof-mounted LiDAR, Stereo Perception Cameras, RTK-GNSS, IMU.
  • Actuation: Drive-by-Wire (DBW) Throttle, Brakes, and Steer.
  • Safety Override: Dual-redundant instant torque driver override.
