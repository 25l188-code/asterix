Medical Administration Remote vaccination for wild animals, administered via air, water, or food supply.

Swarm Blueprint

6. Proposed Solution Overview

Electric BAJA Chassis

Tubular steel roll cage built for extreme terrain. High ground clearance and long-travel suspension prevent bottoming out in mud.

Regenerative Braking

Standard EV drive controller captures energy during steep downhill trail descents, extending field operating range by up to 15%.

Edge Autonomy

Local NVIDIA Orin compute maps paths in real time. Quiet zones are mapped as polygon boundaries, restricting throttle response near nests.

Bio-Mimetic VTOL

Carbon fiber wing structures modeled after large birds. Extremely low acoustic footprint avoids trigger startle responses in canopy nesting species.

Shared Airframe

All drones utilize the identical layout to ensure 100% parts modularity. 3 drones are docked on each AEOUV ground hub.

Autonomous Docks

Vehicle roof-mounted charging pads capture drones via guide-rails and charge batteries using weather-sealed magnetic contacts.

6-Magazine Swapper

Internal adaptive cargo shuttle coordinates with electromagnets to swap modular payload pods into drone slots in under 30 seconds.

Dual 5-DOF Robotic Arms

Twin manipulators mounted on AEOUV chassis to drill soil samples, clear trail debris, and siphon water from river banks.

Payload Canisters

Weatherproof pods containing targeted CO₂ vaccine dart launchers, seed hoppers, chemical fluid siphons, and ranger supply kits.

Persistent Tree Sensors

Solar-recharged passive cylinders deployed by drones. Monitor regional pollution and capture audio alerts 24/7.

Sub-GHz LoRa Mesh

Routes signal hops from tree-to-tree where cellular blocks occur, sending alerts to the nearest patrolling AEOUV base.

Hydrological Probes

Submerged sensor pods continuously monitoring river temperature, chemical runoff, and organic contaminants.

How the Solution Meets Each Mandate

Smart Pod night-vision camera grids track species movement. Acoustic arrays identify gunshots and engine signatures. Biomimetic drones run thermal checks to locate herds without creating ground disruption.

EcoWing-U drones drop biodegradable seed pods wrapped in slow-release fertilizer on steep slopes. AEOUV ground manipulators bore into soil to take core samples and inspect root health in dense foliage.

Targeted vaccines are loaded in swappable pods. Drones administer vaccines via aerial spray or micro-darting. Aquatic species are vaccinated via bait drops; land animals utilize dosed water reservoirs.

Passive hydrological Smart Pods sense chemical spikes. Warehouse neural networks back-trace river sensor arrays to identify point-source contamination boundaries in real time.

Baja Chassis

Regen Powertrain

72V nominal LFP pack, 9 kW motor. Regenerates charge during downhill beats.

Edge Compute

NVIDIA Orin NX with 100W power grid. Runs off-road pathfinding and quiet-zone throttle.

Biomimetic Wing

Carbon fiber shell structure mimicking bird shapes. Eliminates standard propeller rotor buzz.

PX4 Autopilot

Embedded visual odometry handles orientation in dense forests during GPS loss.

Universal Bus

Drones are swap-compatible. Any drone can load any modular pod from the vehicle deck.

Adaptive Mission Bay

Holds 6 pods in a rotary carousel. Swaps target canisters in under 30 seconds.

EPM Latches

Electro-permanent magnets hold pods with zero active current. Unlocks instantly on command.

Chamber Seal

Enclosed, waterproof bottom door prevents moisture and river water from penetrating payload logs.

Dual Manipulators

Interactive Tooling

Automated tool changers select siphons, soil augers, or seed drills dynamically.

Force Feedback

Stops arm motion instantly if hitting hard rock, preventing link fractures.

Smart Pod Blueprint

LoRa Gateway

Step 1: Objective Issue

Headquarters releases mission goals (such as monitoring anomalies or planting seeds) to the Fleet Controller system.

Drones deliver vaccines via aerial spraying or food/water bait. In critical cases, animals are tracked via cameras, and vaccines are administered remotely using micro-darting pods. Fecal matter is monitored to detect early disease presence.

If an AEOUV vehicle detects a system fault, it switches to satellite teleoperation. During a complete breakdown, the system dispatches a sister AEOUV to autonomously hook and tow it back to support hubs.

13. Legal Agreement, Warranty, and Maintenance Plan

Liability Terms

Asterix is not liable for wildlife accidents or environmental impacts during autonomous operations. Sensor and visual logs are shared with rangers upon consent.

Warranty Exclusions

Covers components under standard operation. Excludes user modification, battery water immersion beyond IP67 limits, and physical damage from tree falls.

Maintenance dispatch

Routine support is condition-based. Field teams are dispatched to warehouses only when smart sensors report battery failures or diagnostic drops.

Mitigation

14. Risk Assessment and Mitigation

Mitigation: Onboard computing carries autonomous pathfinding logic. V2V wifi mesh networks route data around canyon gaps.

Mitigation: Diagnostics triggers alert. A sister AEOUV vehicle is automatically dispatched to hook and retrieve the broken buggy.

Mitigation: Electronic boards sealed in IP67 heat-damped cases. All wiring leverages military marine grade connectors.

Mitigation: Drones leverage quiet, bio-mimetic wings. Ground vehicles treat nest zones as strict polygon no-go zones.

Mitigation: Network runs locally using sub-GHz LoRa mesh routers, requiring no active cellular/internet links.
