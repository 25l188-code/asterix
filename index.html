System Architecture

Cooperative Swarm Blueprint

AEOUV Ground Hub

  • Edge Computing: Nvidia Jetson Orin Core for local computer vision and swarm task routing.
  • Robotic Arm: 5-DOF manipulator with hot-swappable tools for automatic water/soil sampling.
  • LiDAR Mapping: 360° rotating solid-state LiDAR for off-road self-driving.
  • Power System: 72V Nominal AIS-156 compliant battery pack (mobile power grid).

EcoWing-U Drones

  • Hybrid VTOL: Lift rotors for vertical canopy launch; folding wings + rear pusher for 25km glide range.
  • Universal Airframe: Identical motors, IMUs, and navigation systems to simplify spares and logistics.
  • Redundancy: Dynamic hand-off: if one drone fails, the next docked drone takes over its mission pod.
  • Permanent Sensors: Onboard thermal and RGB cameras with edge animal recognition.

Sensory Suite

  • Perception: Real-time visual tracking of wildlife and poachers under heavy jungle canopy.
  • Environment: Meteorological monitoring (CO₂, humidity, temperature, PM2.5, rain metrics).
  • Telemetry: Mesh networking node coordinating AEOUV-to-Drone and Drone-to-Drone data streams.

Automated Pod Swapper

  • Internal Shuttle: High-precision linear rail shuttle translates pods from magazine to drone landing docks.
  • Weatherproof Seal: Sealed internal bay protecting sensitive pods from jungle mud, rain, and humidity.
  • Hot-Swapping: Automatic payload attachment completed in under 30 seconds.

Mission Pod Types

  • Vaccination Pod: Pressurized CO₂ dart launcher with smart targeting optics.
  • Seed Planting Pod: Drops biodegradable seed capsules wrapped in slow-release fertilizer.
  • Sampling Pod: Deep canopy air collector and remote river siphon.
  • Emergency Delivery: Delivers medical kits and beacons to rangers.

Magnetic Payload Dock

  • Electro-magnets: Provides rigid structural lock without moving parts.
  • Interface Contacts: Instant electrical power coupling and gigabit fiber communication bus.
  • Alignment Sensors: Guide pins ensure micrometer accuracy during docking.

Persistent Nodes

  • Passive Monitoring: Ultra-low power sensors placed in trees or rivers to capture forest activity.
  • Acoustic Listening: Detects gunshots, chainsaws, and animal distress noises 24/7.
  • Hydrological Probes: Submerged nodes measuring water pH, temperature, and chemicals.

Self-Healing Mesh

  • Line-of-Sight Bypass: In dense canopy, signal hops from pod to pod to circumvent blocking geography.
  • AEOUV Integration: Alerts hop to the nearest trail-bound AEOUV to trigger target drone launches.
  • Long Battery Life: Low-power sleep states keep pods functional for over 12 months.

Deployment

  • Drone Dropped: EcoWing-U drones fly to targeted valleys and plant pods using mechanical grippers.
  • Diagnostics: Ground hub runs daily remote checks to monitor battery levels of deployed pods.

Zero Footprint

  • Trail Bound: Heavy vehicles stay on logging roads, preventing tire tracks and soil erosion in sensitive zones.
  • Quiet Patrol: Extremely silent electric motors avoid disrupting animal nesting cycles.

Operational Speed

  • Rapid Response: Drones bypass river obstacles and vertical drops, arriving at hotspots in minutes.
  • Continuous Audits: Seamless carbon offset verification via automated tree and soil data tracking.
